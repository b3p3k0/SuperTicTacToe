Rules

  1. Player 1 (X) starts on any square of any board.
  2. The little square you pick (1–9) chooses the big board your opponent must play next.
  3. If that destination board is full, they may pick any board with open spaces.
  4. Captured big boards stay live—your opponent can steal them back until that mini-board fills, so keep pressure on both attack and defense.
  5. First to capture three big boards in a row wins the whole game. If everything fills up, it’s a draw.
